About Us

Established in 2018, NDD Tech is a premier provider of auxiliary, automation, electrical, and HVAC contracting & subcontracting services, as well as aluminum and glass works. With a strong commitment to excellence, we continuously deliver remarkable technical support and innovative solutions tailored to meet our clients' diverse needs.

At NDD Tech, we are guided by our three core values:

  • Commitment – We empower our people to excel, ensuring we not only meet but exceed client expectations.
  • Efficiency – We prioritize precision and productivity, delivering top-tier services without compromising quality.
  • Innovation – We embrace continuous learning and cutting-edge advancements to provide progressive, future-ready solutions.
